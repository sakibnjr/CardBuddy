Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a virtual card?

You can create a virtual card by signing up for an account and following the steps in your dashboard to create a new card.

What payment methods are supported for top-ups?

We support Bkash, Nagad, and various other mobile banking services for top-ups.

Is my information secure?

Yes, we use advanced encryption and security measures to protect your personal and financial information.

Can I use the virtual card internationally?

Yes, our virtual cards are accepted globally wherever MasterCard or Visa is accepted.
